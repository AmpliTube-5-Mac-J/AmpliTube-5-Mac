## 💡 Overview

<a href="#ampliTube5">AmpliTube 5</a> serves guitarists and bassists across home recording, professional studio work, and live performance who want professional-quality amplifier tones accessible on a Mac without the expense, space, and volume requirements of physical tube amplifier rigs.

The <a href="#ampliTube5">home recording use case</a> is where AmpliTube 5 has the most immediate impact — guitarists who need professional amp tones at apartment-compatible volume levels, recording direct into a Mac interface with AmpliTube 5 delivering the complete amp and cabinet simulation on the recorded track. The gap between a raw DI guitar signal and a processed amp tone makes AmpliTube 5 essential for home recording quality. The <a href="#ampliTube5">studio session recording context</a> uses AmpliTube 5 for controlled, noiseless direct guitar recording — session guitarists who want consistent, repeatable tones across sessions without the acoustic and logistical complexity of physical amplifier miking.

The <a href="#ampliTube5">performing musician use case</a> runs AmpliTube 5 on a Mac in the signal chain between guitar and PA system — using software amp modeling to get professional stage tones without a physical amplifier on stage, reducing both equipment transport and stage volume. <a href="#ampliTube5">CustomShop gear expansion</a> through IK Multimedia's in-app store extends the base gear library with additional officially modeled amplifiers, cabinets, effects, and microphones as the player's tonal needs expand.

<a href="#ampliTube5">Preset system for complete rigs</a> saves the full signal chain — every amp setting, cabinet choice, microphone position, and effects configuration — as a named preset that recalls the complete tone instantly for live use or session consistency. <a href="#ampliTube5">Low-latency real-time performance mode</a> reduces processing delay to sub-10ms levels that allow comfortable real-time guitar playing through AmpliTube 5 with standard audio interfaces.

<a href="#ampliTube5">Standalone and plugin flexibility</a> means AmpliTube 5 operates as a self-contained practice and performance tool or as an AU/VST plugin within any compatible Mac DAW for recording integration — the same gear library and tone settings available in both environments. <a href="#ampliTube5">IK Multimedia hardware pairing</a> with AXE I/O and other IK audio interfaces provides optimized low-latency guitar-to-Mac connectivity for the most demanding real-time playing contexts.

The TONEX integration moves AmpliTube 5 forward from traditional modeling to AI capture. Classic amp modeling derives tone from circuit simulation of an idealized version of an amplifier design. TONEX Captures are trained on audio from a specific real amplifier unit — capturing the idiosyncrasies, breakup characteristics, and sonic personality of that particular piece of hardware in a model that loads directly into AmpliTube 5 alongside the traditionally modeled gear.

## 💬 FAQ

<details>
<summary>Can I build a signal chain with effects before and after the amp?</summary>

Yes. The signal chain editor places effects in any position — before the amp input, in the effects loop, or after the cabinet in the rack section.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does AmpliTube 5 include bass amp models?</summary>

Yes. Bass amplifier heads, bass speaker cabinets, and bass-appropriate effects are available alongside the guitar gear models.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the CustomShop?</summary>

The CustomShop is IK Multimedia's in-app store for purchasing additional officially modeled gear — specific amplifiers, cabinets, effects, and microphones beyond the base installation.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I save my complete rig configuration?</summary>

Yes. The preset system saves the complete signal chain — every amp, cabinet, microphone, and effect setting — as a named preset for instant recall.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does AmpliTube 5 pair with IK Multimedia audio interfaces?</summary>

Yes. AXE I/O and other IK Multimedia interfaces provide optimized low-latency connectivity for guitar recording and real-time performance through AmpliTube 5.

</details>
